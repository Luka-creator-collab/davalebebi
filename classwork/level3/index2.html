Lamborghini

Lamborghini is an Italian luxury manufacturer of high-performance supercars and SUVs based in Sant'Agata Bolognese, Italy. Founded in 1963 by Ferruccio Lamborghini to compete with Ferrari, the company is now a subsidiary of Audi, which is part of the Volkswagen Group.

Ferrari

Ferrari is a legendary Italian manufacturer of high-performance luxury sports cars based in Maranello, Italy. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, the brand is globally recognized for its "Prancing Horse" logo and its unrivaled heritage in Formula One racing.

Bugatti

Bugatti is a French luxury hyper-performance automobile manufacturer founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti in Molsheim, France. The brand is world-renowned for producing some of the fastest and most exclusive production cars, such as the Veyron and Chiron
